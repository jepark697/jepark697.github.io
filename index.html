About me

As a Designer...

I’m a Senior at UC San Diego majoring in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction. And, becasue I couldn't pick just one, I'm minoring in both Design, and Entrepreneurship & Innovation.

Currently, I'm working as a UX/UI Designer Intern at ServiceNow on the IT Operational Management team. My team creates products that allow IT Professsionals to streamline their work by, for example, helping IT Technicians see which issues are the most severe. There, I've been fortunate enough to work on everything from wireframing to helping to interview users. Previously, I've also worked as a Research Assistant in the Contextual Robotics Labratory where I helped design robotics for Older Adults. But, you can learn more about my work history at LinkedIn or read my resume.

As you can see by my work history, a lot of the work I do revolves around creating designs that help others. I'm a huge advocate of designing for inclusivity and making products that make my users' lives just a little bit easier.
